SCHOOL

Schools in Sikkim are located in different terrain and during monsoon season it's very difficult to reach every school to get data. In govt sector sometimes we need data immediately in such scenario creating data is difficult. This Web-Portal which can store data of all student. The data storage is in school level, block level, district level and state level.

BLOCK

The Block comprises of many schools and block can access all the data from a school. Block can also send regulation to the schools and send custom their own report to the higher level for the convenience

DISTRICT

District serve a major node in information flow. District have ability to access and control the flow of information. District serve directly to the State of Sikkim.

STATE

The State of Sikkim have ultimate authority on the database and have ability to access the data on individul student of whole state. This allow state to make better policies and give the instant feedback on reports received.
